# Mathematics P1-P6 MOE Curriculum (2021 Syllabus, Updated Oct 2025)

Source: MOE Singapore Primary Mathematics Syllabus
Applicable: PSLE 2026 onwards

---

## P1 (Primary 1)

### Number & Algebra
**1. Whole Numbers to 100**
- Counting, reading, writing numbers to 100
- Place value: tens and ones
- Compare and order numbers
- Number patterns, number bonds
- Methods: Ten-frames, Number line

**2. Addition & Subtraction within 100**
- Addition within 100
- Subtraction within 100
- Mental calculation strategies
- Word problems (adding, taking away, comparing)
- Methods: Number bonds, Part-whole model, Count on/back

**3. Multiplication & Division Basics**
- Multiplication within 40 (2, 5, 10 times)
- Division within 20
- Equal groups concept, sharing equally
- Methods: Grouping objects, Repeated addition, Array model

**4. Money**
- Recognise Singapore coins and notes
- Count money in dollars and cents
- Add and subtract money
- Methods: Counting on strategy

### Measurement & Geometry
**5. Length**
- Compare and measure lengths in cm
- Use a ruler correctly
- Methods: Direct comparison, Non-standard units

**6. Time**
- Read time on clock to 5 minutes
- Use a.m. and p.m.
- Duration of events
- Days of the week, months
- Methods: Clock reading strategy

**7. 2D Shapes**
- Rectangle, square, triangle, circle
- Identify shapes in real life
- Form figures with shapes
- Methods: Shape sorting

### Statistics
**8. Picture Graphs**
- Read picture graphs
- Answer questions from graphs
- Compare data
- Methods: Counting from pictures

## P2 (Primary 2)

### Number & Algebra
**1. Whole Numbers to 1,000**
- Place value: hundreds, tens, ones
- Compare and order to 1,000
- Odd and even numbers
- Number patterns
- Methods: Place value chart, Number line

**2. Addition & Subtraction (3 digits)**
- Addition with regrouping
- Subtraction with regrouping
- Mental strategies
- Word problems (2 steps)
- Methods: Column method, Part-whole model

**3. Multiplication & Division Tables**
- Times tables: 2, 3, 4, 5, 10
- Division as grouping/sharing
- Multiply and divide within tables
- Word problems
- Methods: Skip counting, Array model, Equal groups

**4. Fractions**
- Unit fractions (1/2, 1/3, 1/4)
- Like fractions (same denominator)
- Fraction of a whole
- Add/subtract like fractions within 1
- Methods: Fraction bar model, Equal parts of shapes

**5. Money (Decimal Notation)**
- Dollars and cents in decimal
- Convert between $ and ¢
- Add and subtract money
- Word problems with money
- Methods: Convert to cents then calculate

### Measurement & Geometry
**6. Mass & Volume**
- Measure mass in kg and g
- Measure volume in L
- Compare and order measurements
- Addition/subtraction of measurements
- Methods: Reading scales

**7. Time (Minutes)**
- Read time to the minute
- Convert hours ↔ minutes
- Duration calculations, Timeline
- Methods: Clock face counting, Timeline method

**8. 2D & 3D Shapes**
- Cube, cuboid, cone, cylinder, sphere
- Identify in real life
- Shape patterns
- Faces, edges, vertices
- Methods: Shape classification

### Statistics
**9. Picture Graphs with Scales**
- Picture graphs with scales (1 picture = 2, 5, etc.)
- Interpret and compare data
- Solve problems from graphs
- Methods: Multiply by scale value

## P3 (Primary 3)

### Number & Algebra
**1. Whole Numbers to 10,000**
- Place value to 10,000
- Compare and order
- Number patterns
- Rounding to nearest 10/100
- Methods: Place value chart

**2. Addition & Subtraction (4 digits)**
- Add/subtract up to 4 digits
- Estimation by rounding
- Checking answers
- Multi-step word problems
- Methods: Column method, Estimation, Model drawing

**3. Multiplication & Division (Extended)**
- Times tables: 6, 7, 8, 9
- Multiply 3 digits by 1 digit
- Divide 3 digits by 1 digit
- Remainder concept
- Methods: Long multiplication, Long division

**4. Equivalent Fractions**
- Equivalent fractions
- Simplest form
- Compare and order fractions
- Add/subtract related fractions
- Methods: Fraction wall, Common denominator

**5. Money (Decimals)**
- Add/subtract decimal money amounts
- Estimation with money
- Multi-step money problems
- Methods: Align decimal points

### Measurement & Geometry
**6. Length & Volume (Conversions)**
- km and m conversions
- mL and L conversions
- Add/subtract with mixed units
- Word problems
- Methods: Unit conversion table

**7. Time (24-hour clock)**
- 24-hour clock
- Convert 12h ↔ 24h
- Seconds
- Duration with start/end time
- Methods: Timeline method, Add/subtract time

**8. Area & Perimeter**
- Area in cm² and m²
- Perimeter of rectangles/squares
- Area of rectangles/squares
- Relationship between area and perimeter
- Methods: Count squares, L × W, 2(L+W)

**9. Angles**
- Right angle = 90°
- Angles greater/smaller than right angle
- Identifying angles in shapes
- Methods: Set square to check right angles

**10. Perpendicular & Parallel Lines**
- Perpendicular lines (meet at 90°)
- Parallel lines (never meet)
- Identify in shapes and real life
- Methods: Using set square and ruler

### Statistics
**11. Bar Graphs**
- Read bar graphs with scales
- Compare data from bar graphs
- Draw bar graphs
- Solve problems from graphs
- Methods: Read scale carefully, Align bars to gridlines

## P4 (Primary 4)

### Number & Algebra
**1. Whole Numbers to 100,000**
- Place value to 100,000
- Rounding to nearest 1,000
- Factors and multiples
- Common factors and multiples
- Methods: Factor tree, Listing method

**2. Four Operations (4 digits ÷ 2 digits)**
- Multiply up to 4 digits by 2 digits
- Divide up to 4 digits by 2 digits
- Order of operations
- Multi-step word problems
- Methods: Long multiplication, Long division, Model drawing

**3. Fractions (Mixed & Improper)**
- Mixed numbers ↔ improper fractions
- Add/subtract unlike fractions
- Fraction of a set
- Word problems with fractions
- Methods: Common denominator, Model drawing

**4. Decimals (3 places)**
- Decimals to 3 places
- Express fractions as decimals
- Add/subtract/multiply decimals
- Compare and order decimals
- Methods: Place value chart, Align decimal points

### Measurement & Geometry
**5. Area & Perimeter (Composite)**
- Composite figures from rectangles/squares
- Break into parts strategy
- Find unknown sides
- Word problems
- Methods: Cut and add, Big minus small

**6. Angles (Measuring & Drawing)**
- Use protractor to measure angles
- Draw angles of given size
- Angles on a straight line = 180°
- Angles at a point = 360°
- Methods: Protractor reading, Unknown angle calculation

**7. Rectangle & Square Properties**
- All angles 90°
- Opposite sides equal (rectangle)
- All sides equal (square)
- Diagonal properties
- Methods: Property identification

**8. Line Symmetry**
- Line of symmetry
- Identify symmetric shapes
- Draw lines of symmetry
- Complete symmetric figures
- Methods: Folding test, Mirror line

**9. Nets of 3D Shapes**
- Net of a cube (11 types)
- Net of a cuboid
- Identify which nets fold into shapes
- Surface area connection
- Methods: Cut and fold, Visualisation

### Statistics
**10. Tables, Line Graphs & Pie Charts**
- Read and complete tables
- Read line graphs (trends)
- Read pie charts (fractions/percentages)
- Compare data across graph types
- Methods: Identify trends, Calculate fractions of total

## P5 (Primary 5)

### Number & Algebra
**1. Whole Numbers to 10 Million**
- Numbers to 10,000,000
- Multiply/divide by 10, 100, 1,000
- Order of operations (BODMAS)
- Use of brackets
- Methods: Place value chart, BODMAS rule

**2. Fractions (× and ÷)**
- Multiply proper/improper/mixed fractions
- Divide fractions by whole number
- Fraction of a quantity
- Multi-step word problems
- Methods: Model drawing, Multiply numerators/denominators

**3. Decimals (All Operations)**
- Multiply/divide decimals
- Convert fractions ↔ decimals
- Estimation with decimals
- Multi-step problems
- Methods: Align decimal points, Equivalent fractions

**4. Percentage**
- Express as percentage
- Fraction ↔ decimal ↔ percentage
- Find percentage of a quantity
- Discount, GST, interest problems
- Methods: Convert to fraction first, ÷ 100 or × 100

**5. Rate**
- Rate as quantity per unit
- Calculate unit rate
- Compare rates
- Word problems with rate
- Methods: Unitary method, Table of values

### Measurement & Geometry
**6. Area of Triangles & Volume**
- Area of triangle = ½ × base × height
- Identify base and height
- Volume of cube/cuboid = L × W × H
- Volume in cm³ and m³, Volume and capacity (litres)
- Methods: ½ × b × h formula, L × W × H formula

**7. Angles (Lines & Points)**
- Angles on line = 180°
- Angles at point = 360°
- Vertically opposite angles are equal
- Finding unknown angles
- Methods: Angle relationships, Setting up equations

**8. Triangle Properties**
- Angle sum of triangle = 180°
- Isosceles: 2 equal sides and angles
- Equilateral: all equal (60° each)
- Right-angled: one 90° angle
- Methods: 180° - known angles, Identify triangle type

**9. Quadrilateral Properties**
- Parallelogram: opposite sides parallel and equal
- Rhombus: all sides equal
- Trapezium: one pair parallel sides
- Angle sum = 360°
- Methods: Property identification, Unknown angle finding

### Statistics
**10. Data Analysis (Extended)**
- Multi-step data problems
- Compare across data sets
- Draw conclusions from data
- Communicate findings
- Methods: Read carefully, Show working

## P6 (Primary 6) — PSLE Year

### Number & Algebra
**1. Fractions (Division)**
- Divide proper/improper fractions
- Divide mixed numbers
- Complex multi-step problems
- Model drawing for fraction problems
- Methods: Keep-Change-Flip, Model drawing

**2. Percentage (Increase/Decrease)**
- Percentage increase/decrease
- Find percentage change
- Find original value given %
- GST, discount, profit/loss
- Methods: Original = 100%, New = 100% ± change%

**3. Ratio** ⭐ Key PSLE topic
- Express ratio a:b
- Equivalent ratios (simplify)
- Ratio and fraction connection
- Unchanged quantity problems
- Total unchanged problems
- Methods: Model drawing, Unchanged quantity method, Units method

**4. Algebra** 🆕 New from 2026
- Use letters for unknowns
- Write algebraic expressions
- Evaluate expressions
- Solve simple equations (e.g., 3x + 5 = 20)
- Methods: Substitution, Balancing method, Working backwards

### Measurement & Geometry
**5. Circles** ⭐ Key PSLE topic
- Circumference = π × d = 2πr
- Area = πr²
- Semicircle and quarter circle
- Composite figures with circles
- Methods: Use π = 3.14 or 22/7, Break into parts

**6. Volume (Extended)**
- Volume of composite cuboids
- Find unknown length/width/height
- Relationship: volume, base area, height
- Word problems
- Methods: V = base area × height, Working backwards

**7. Geometry (Composite Shapes)**
- Angle properties in composite figures
- Identify parallel lines and angles
- Use multiple angle properties together
- PSLE-style geometry problems
- Methods: Step-by-step angle finding, Mark known angles first

### Statistics
**8. Average** ⭐ Key PSLE topic
- Average = Total ÷ Number
- Find total from average
- Find missing value given average
- Changing average problems
- Methods: Total = Average × Number, Before-after method
